In case you’re wondering

Questions & Answers

What is the dress code?

Black tie optional. We encourage formal attire to match the elegance of the evening — think floor-length gowns, cocktail dresses, or dark suits and tuxedos. Deep jewel tones and winter whites are especially welcome.

Can I bring a plus one?

Due to venue capacity, we are only able to accommodate those guests formally named on the invitation. If you received a plus one, it will be noted on your invitation.

Are children welcome?

While we love your little ones, our wedding will be an adults-only celebration. We hope this gives you a wonderful excuse for a night out.

Is there parking at the venue?

Complimentary valet parking will be available at both the ceremony and reception venues. Details will be shared closer to the date.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

Please let us know about any dietary needs on your RSVP. We are committed to making sure every guest is well taken care of at dinner.

Will the ceremony and reception be indoors or outdoors?

Both the ceremony and reception will be held indoors — no need to worry about the winter weather. Cozy, candlelit, and warm.
